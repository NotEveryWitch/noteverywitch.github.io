How do I become a patient in the medbay?
There are currently four ways to be admitted to the medbay:
1) Eat Molly's food from the fridge.
2) Climb up fast on the ladder.
3) Increase the temperature using the machine in the cargo bay.
4) Eat the strange substance from the black lunchbox in the kitchen fridge.
How do I complete the ladder event?
Wait until the girl is near the ladder, click on the ladder, and choose the "Let her go first" option.
How do I complete the salt event (Jenny's lunch)?
Wait until Jenny sits down for lunch in the kitchen and join her. After that, you will be able to hide the salt and ask Jenny to look for it during her next lunch.
How do I turn on the alarm?
Knock on Molly's shower door a few times, until she tells you that if the ship went off course, the alarm on the bridge would be blaring. After that, turn on the alarm on the bridge and choose "Step away." It will keep flashing after you leave.
