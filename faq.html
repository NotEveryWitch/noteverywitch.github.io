Tap any question to see the answer.

(!) Most events depend on your relationship level, which must be increased to progress further.

When is the next update?
Updates are released roughly once a month. The new build is first released for Officer-tier patrons and higher, while the previous build goes public. Two weeks after that, it becomes available to Crew Member subscribers.
Where can I report bugs?
Please report any bugs to the #bugs channel on Discord.
Thanks!
How do I increase the relationship level?

Try complimenting the girl.
Try giving her gifts.

Try picking perverted options 😳
How do I become a patient in the medbay?
There are currently three ways to be admitted to the medbay:
1) Eat Molly's food from the fridge.
2) Rush up the ladder.
3) Increase the temperature using the machine in the cargo bay.
How do I complete the ladder event?
Wait until the girl is near the ladder, click on the ladder, and choose the "Let her go first" option.
How do I complete the salt event (Jenny's lunch)?
Wait until Jenny sits down for lunch in the kitchen and join her. After that, you will be able to hide the salt and ask Jenny to look for it during her next lunch.
